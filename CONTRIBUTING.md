# Contributing

Contributions to ZPE-FT are evidence-first.

## Ground Rules

- Negative results are valid artifacts.
- Do not upgrade claims without proof.
- Do not remove failing or inconclusive evidence to create a cleaner story.
- Keep changes scoped. One concern per change.

## Before You Open A Change

1. Read [README.md](README.md), [AUDITOR_PLAYBOOK.md](AUDITOR_PLAYBOOK.md), and [PUBLIC_AUDIT_LIMITS.md](PUBLIC_AUDIT_LIMITS.md).
2. If you touch claim-bearing logic, attach concrete before/after evidence.
3. If you find a portability defect, prefer repo-relative paths over machine-local paths.
